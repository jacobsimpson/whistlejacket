Our Services

The training offered at WJT is about learning to listen, learning to be softer, learning to be mindful and thoughtful. This means taking things slowly and trying to understand things more from the horse’s perspective. Whether we’re doing ground work, riding, or training the goal is careful and methodical work where we strive to understand our horses and do our best for them. The experience of horses is unlike any other in life as they offer us relationship, softness, beauty, simplicity, wrapped up in mystery and wildness and strength that touches something deep in the soul. And the amazing part is that horses are so desperate to communicate with us, but we have to be willing to listen and to take the time to let them teach us. Whistle Jacket Training offers only the best in training and instruction for all horses and all riders. With over 20 years experience our goal is to provide each individual with a customized program to fit their needs.

Groundwork

All instruction and training begins on the ground because this is where the relationship is first established. Spending time doing intentional exercises from the ground gives riders a chance to observe how their horse thinks and moves. For the horse these exercises engage the thinking side of their brain, rather than the reactive side, which helps them be more confident and relaxed which is a much better starting place for any riding.

Groundwork includes basic leading exercises, maneuverability exercises to move forehand and hindquarters, lateral work for suppling using leg-yield and shoulder-in, thoughtful exercises with obstacles like poles, barrels and cones, also lunging, and liberty work in the round pen.

Lessons

Riding instruction is based in the classical discipline of dressage with a focus on slow development for both horse and rider. We spend time on building a strong foundation in the seat which is the beginning and the end of all things but especially balance, feel and communication with the horse. To facilitate this work riders begin lessons on the lunge line (often without stirrups and reins) learning to feel and engage their own bodies in a way the horse can understand and connect with.

Training

Classical dressage training is all about making horses more rideable which means methodical work that develops both mental and physical balance and softness. Our training program is customized to each horse and owner combination and includes ground work and riding sessions appropriate to each horse. Time and care are taken to build physical fitness and mental understanding while also fostering willingness and softness. We want horses who are partners and who enjoy the riding experience!

About Us

For the next great ride

Whistle Jacket Training officially began in 2006 as a small operation with a young trainer getting her feet wet. I had about 10 years of experience under my belt at the time and was ready to launch into a career that has taken many interesting turns. Since then the business has grown and matured into a full time training and teaching operation. I have done some successful showing over the last few years, but my primary focus is on my students and horses at home. I find joy in the journey and the promise of growth. When my students have success, I am right there with them. Most importantly we never stop learning. I am passionate about what I do and working to develop my knowledge and skills to better understand and communicate.
